Website Indexing

Crawl and index your website so the assistant can answer from your real pages, not generic guesses.

What it does

Website Indexing lets SAK ingest your website content and use it to answer customer questions. This is ideal for FAQs, product pages, policies, and informational pages.

Key capabilities

  • • Crawl your website and store searchable content.
  • • Use indexed pages to answer customer questions with higher accuracy.
  • • Keep a visible list of indexed URLs and search status in the dashboard.

Typical use cases

  • • “Do you ship to my city?” / “What’s the return policy?”
  • • “What are the specs for this item?”
  • • “How do I place an order?”
