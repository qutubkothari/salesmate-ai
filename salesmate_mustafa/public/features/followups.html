Follow-ups

Convert ongoing chats into a structured pipeline—schedule next actions, track status, and move leads into Customers.

What it does

Follow-ups ensure every interested customer gets a next step. Your team can schedule, update, and close follow-ups directly from the dashboard.

Key capabilities

  • • Create follow-ups from conversations and keep a clear queue.
  • • Track status (pending/completed) so nothing is forgotten.
  • • Add a follow-up contact directly into Customers for long-term CRM.
  • • Keep history and context aligned with chat activity.

Typical flow

  1. 1) Customer asks for quote or shows buying intent.
  2. 2) Team schedules a follow-up with notes.
  3. 3) Follow-up is completed or converted into an order/customer record.
