Broadcast

Run WhatsApp campaigns to your customer lists and track results so your team knows who to follow up with.

What it does

Broadcast helps you send campaign messages at scale and track delivery outcomes—turning WhatsApp into a measurable growth channel.

Key capabilities

  • • Create campaign messages (text and optional media).
  • • Send to selected customer segments.
  • • Track status and identify who needs retry/follow-up.
  • • Convert responses into Follow-ups or Orders.

Best practice workflow

  1. 1) Prepare campaign copy + offer.
  2. 2) Choose target segment (from Customers).
  3. 3) Send broadcast and monitor results.
  4. 4) Create follow-ups for interested responders.
