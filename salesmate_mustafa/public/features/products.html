Products

Manage your product catalog, SKUs, pricing, and categories so the AI can quote accurately and the team can operate from one source of truth.

What it does

Products is your catalog management hub. It’s designed to support fast quoting on WhatsApp and clean operations in the dashboard.

Key capabilities

  • • Create/edit products with SKU, price, units/carton, and metadata.
  • • Search by name/SKU for quick answering and order building.
  • • Bulk add products for fast onboarding.
  • • Categorization support and management workflows.

Why it matters for WhatsApp

  • • Accurate replies: AI answers use the same data your dashboard shows.
  • • Faster conversion: customers get price + availability without waiting for a human.
  • • Fewer mistakes: consistent pricing/pack sizes reduce order issues.
