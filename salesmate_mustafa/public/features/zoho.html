Zoho Integration

Optional Zoho Books integration to sync items/customers/orders and generate invoices—useful when your back-office runs on Zoho.

What it does

When enabled, Zoho integration connects SAK’s WhatsApp-driven operations with Zoho Books workflows, helping maintain consistency between chat orders and accounting/inventory systems.

Key capabilities

  • • Sync items/products from Zoho to keep catalog aligned.
  • • Sync customers and match contacts by phone when possible.
  • • Create sales orders/invoices in Zoho as part of checkout workflows (depending on configuration).

When to use it

  • • Your finance/inventory team uses Zoho Books as the source of truth.
  • • You want automated invoice creation after order confirmation.
  • • You need periodic syncing to avoid manual duplication.
