## 2) What the Service Does
The Service helps businesses automate WhatsApp sales conversations using AI and a web dashboard. It can process messages, store conversation history, manage product catalogs, create orders, schedule follow-ups, and send broadcast messages.

## 3) Information We Collect / Process
Depending on configuration and usage, we may process:

### A. Business (Tenant) Data
- Business name and configuration
- Account identifiers (e.g., email/credentials) for dashboard access
- Subscription/trial metadata (if enabled)

### B. Customer Data
- WhatsApp phone number or chat identifier
- Customer message content and metadata (timestamps/message ids)
- Customer profile fields provided during checkout or support flows (e.g., shipping address)
- Tax-related fields such as GST preference and GST number, if provided

### C. Commerce Data
- Products/catalog data entered by the business
- Carts and orders, including order items and totals

### D. Knowledge Data (Optional)
- Uploaded documents
- Website content crawled and indexed by the business

### E. Technical Data
- Server logs and diagnostic data

## 4) How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Provide and operate the Service (responding to messages, managing workflows)
- Maintain conversation context and customer profiles
- Create and manage orders and follow-ups
- Run broadcast campaigns when initiated by business operators
- Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues

## 5) AI Processing and Third Parties
The Service may use third-party providers to operate core functionality:
- **AI provider (e.g., OpenAI):** to generate responses and embeddings.
- **Database provider (e.g., Supabase/Postgres):** to store tenant data.
- **Cloud storage provider (optional):** to store documents/assets.

These providers process data only as needed to deliver the Service.

## 6) Data Retention
We retain data for as long as needed to provide the Service to the business tenant, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods can vary by deployment and tenant requirements.

## 7) Data Sharing
