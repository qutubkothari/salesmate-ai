Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 26, 2026

SAK AI ("we," "us," or "our") is a registered trading entity in the United Arab Emirates. We are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the SAK AI WhatsApp Automation tool, we collect and process the following data:

  • Account Data: Business name, phone number (WhatsApp ID), and email address.
  • Transaction Data: Order details, inventory levels, and pricing information synced with our system.
  • Usage Data: Metadata regarding message volume and automation performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to:

  • Provide the WhatsApp automation service (Auto-replies, Order processing).
  • Sync inventory between your database and WhatsApp.
  • Calculate dynamic pricing (including VAT) for your customers.

3. Data Processing & Cross-Border Transfer

SAK AI operates in Dubai, UAE. However, our technology infrastructure is powered by our technical partner, SAK Solutions, based in India.

By using our service, you explicitly consent to your data being processed on secure servers located in India and the UAE for the purpose of service delivery. We ensure that our partners adhere to strict data security standards compatible with UAE law.

4. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade encryption for all data in transit (between WhatsApp and our servers) and at rest. We do not sell your customer data to third parties.

5. Your Rights

Under UAE law, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about your business.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
